The Cost of Teaching

Experience the Realities of Teaching in the Bay Area

South San Francisco Unified School District loses nearly 14% of its high school teachers annually. Want to know why? Play this game and gain a deeper understanding of the challenges that Bay Area educators face on a daily basis.

About Game

This game is based on real reporting and interviews conducted with former and current teachers at South San Francisco High School.

You earned tenure!

Tenure provides job security for teachers who have demonstrated their ability to effectively educate their students, meaning they cannot be fired without due process.

Critics of tenure, however, argue that it can protect ineffective teachers, making it difficult for schools to remove underperforming teachers.
