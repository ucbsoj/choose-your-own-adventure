The Cost of Teaching

Experience the Realities of Teaching in the Bay Area

Every year, about 14% of high school teachers who work in South San Francisco Unified School District leave. Play this game to understand the real challenges that high school teachers in the Bay Area face throughout their career.

About Game

This game is based on real reporting and interviews conducted with former and current teachers at South San Francisco High School.
Variables Impacted:

Money: To save up for a future house, you annually deposit your remaining funds into this savings account dedicated to that goal.

Energy: The energy and time spent on things related to your job.

Motivation: This refers to your motivation to stay in the teaching profession.

You earned tenure!

Tenure provides job security for teachers who have demonstrated their ability to effectively educate their students, meaning they cannot be fired without due process. Critics of tenure, however, argue that it can protect ineffective teachers, making it difficult for schools to remove underperforming teachers.
